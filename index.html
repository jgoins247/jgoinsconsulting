Higher Education · Healthcare

Consumer Journey Audit — College of Health Care Professions

A prospective-student-perspective audit of CHCP's full digital journey vs. two key competitors, from Google search through lead form submission.

Key Findings

  • Client owned the #1 Google Local Pack position for key program searches — direct competitors were completely absent
  • Post-submit experience had a critical gap: polished confirmation flow on one path, rough experience on the program page where most search traffic actually lands
  • Follow-up email landing in Promotions with no clear next steps — a significant enrollment leakage point
  • Social presence significantly behind competitors for prospective student engagement across TikTok and Instagram
  • Navigation inconsistencies between approved wireframes and hi-fi designs identified and documented for dev alignment
